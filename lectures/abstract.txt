
APAS-VERUS: AI Paired Proof Engineering Techniques and Experience

 Brian G. Milnes briangmilnes@gmail.com

 This talk will focus on my experience using LLM AIs to generate the Rust code for its
parallel algorithms and then verifying it in Verus. I overview on the time, the costs, and
the software engineering practice of using programmatic techniques to drive the LLMs
which increase the quality of the code and reduce the costs of validation.

 Then I'll compare and contrast my experience hand-proving in F* with Verus's currently
fast moving targets. And discuss the practicality of targeting the trusted code bases
given the rapidly expanding of ability of LLMs in penetrating code.
