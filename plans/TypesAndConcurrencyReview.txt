EdgeLit!, EdgeList!, PairList!: dead macros. -! Get rid of.

manually. Either adopt HashOrd everywhere or drop it. It saves ~15 chars per bound. -! Adopt everywhere.

uses it. Could be useful for reduce/scan but isn't adopted. -! delete.

PredSt: used in Chap41/43 set/table filter operations. Chap21 seq filter uses
raw Fn(&T) -> bool instead. Should filter operations standardize on PredSt? -! Yes.
