Source: https://devel.isa-afp.org/browser_info/current/AFP/Amortized_Complexity/Splay_Heap_Analysis.html

Amortized analysis of splay heap operations.

POTENTIAL FUNCTION
------------------

φ(t) = log₂(size1(t))

Φ(Leaf) = 0
Φ(Node l a r) = Φ(l) + Φ(r) + φ(Node l a r)

AMORTIZED BOUNDS
-----------------

  U Empty     []  = 0
  U (Insert _) [t] = 3 * log₂(size1(t) + 1) + 1
  U Del_min    [t] = 2 * φ(t) + 1

KEY LEMMAS
----------

amor_partition:
  T_partition(p, t) + Φ(l) + Φ(r) - Φ(t) ≤ 2 * log₂(size1(t)) + 1
  where partition(p, t) = (l, r)

amor_del_min:
  T_del_min(t) + Φ(del_min(t)) - Φ(t) ≤ 2 * φ(t) + 1

PROOF STRUCTURE
---------------

The partition function is the analogue of splay for heaps. It splits a BST
around a pivot, producing two sub-BSTs. The amortized cost analysis follows
the same potential method as splay trees:

- zig_zig and zig_zag lemmas bound the two-level rotation cases
- amor_partition combines them into the overall bound
- insert and del_min are expressed in terms of partition
